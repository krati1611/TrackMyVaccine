Importance of Vaccination


Hepatitis B

Hepatitis B infection; a liver infection caused by the Hepatitis B virus. The Hepatitis B vaccine can be provided alone or in combination vaccines that also help protect against other diseases, as part of routine immunization for infants. Consult with your healthcare professional for further information.


BCG

To be given: Before discharge. BCG is currently the only available TB vaccine and can help protect against tuberculosis infection. The BCG vaccine can be provided as part of routine immunization for infants. Consult with your healthcare professional for further information.


OPV

The polio vaccine can be provided alone or included in combination vaccines that also help protect against other diseases, as part of routine immunization for infants. Unfortunately, there is no cure for the disease. Consult with your healthcare professional for further information.


DTP

The diphtheria, tetanus, and pertussis vaccinations are included in combination vaccines that also help protect against other diseases, as part of routine immunization for infants.Consult with your healthcare professional for further information.
